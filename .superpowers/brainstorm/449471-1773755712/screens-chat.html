What embedding model does Axon use and how does the chunking work?
▶ Thinking · 2.3s
Axon uses TEI (Text Embeddings Inference) for embeddings — a self-hosted HuggingFace service. The chunking is handled by chunk_text() which splits at 2000 chars with 200-char overlap. Each chunk becomes one Qdrant point.
