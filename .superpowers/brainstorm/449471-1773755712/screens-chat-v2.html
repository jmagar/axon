What embedding model does Axon use and how does the chunking work?

Axon
Axon uses TEI (Text Embeddings Inference) for embeddings — a self-hosted HuggingFace service producing 1024-dim vectors. Chunking is handled by chunk_text() which splits at 2000 chars with 200-char overlap. Each chunk = one Qdrant point.
